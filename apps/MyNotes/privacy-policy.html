Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data and ensure complete confidentiality in My Notes app.

Key Principles

Local Storage

All your notes are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to your content.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Complete Anonymity

The app works offline and does not require creating an account or registration.

General Information

This Privacy Policy describes how the "My Notes" app (developed by pasichDev) processes user information. We strive to ensure the highest level of privacy and security for your data.

Last updated: August 30, 2025

What Data We Collect

Personal Data

We DO NOT collect your personal data such as name, email, phone number or address.

Note Content

We DO NOT have access to the content of your notes. All data is stored locally on your device.

Usage Data

We DO NOT track how you use the app or which features you use.

Data Storage

Local Database

All your notes are stored in local SQLite database on your Android device.

Backup

Backup functionality creates a file on your device that you can save yourself.

App Permissions

Storage Access

Used for creating and restoring backups of your notes.

This permission is used exclusively for backup functionality

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We DO NOT share your data

My Notes does not transfer, sell or provide access to your data to third parties, including advertising networks, analytics services or other companies.

Data Security

Encryption

Data is stored using built-in Android security mechanisms.

App Isolation

App data is not accessible to other programs on your device.

Offline Operation

The app works without internet connection.

Data Deletion

When deleting the app, all data is automatically removed from the device.

Child Protection

My Notes app is safe for children to use. We do not collect personal data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When making changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this document. We recommend periodically reviewing this page.
